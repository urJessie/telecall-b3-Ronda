Featured Services

Give Shelter

We shelter for stray animals involves creating a safe, nurturing environment where homeless pets can receive food, medical care, and socialization. Shelters aim to rehabilitate these animals and prepare them for adoption into loving homes, ensuring their well-being and reducing the number of strays on the streets.

Spay/Neuter

These initiatives offer free or low-cost spaying and neutering services to reduce the population of stray animals. By preventing unwanted litters, these programs help control the stray animal population and improve the overall health and quality of life for existing animals.

Trap-Neuter-Return

NR involves humanely trapping stray cats, neutering or spaying them, and then returning them to their original location. This helps manage and gradually reduce feral cat populations while allowing the animals to live out their natural lives without contributing to further overpopulation.

Who We Are
